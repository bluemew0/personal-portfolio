    Character Creation

    I modeled and animated one of my characters (Phoenix) in Blender and you can see my process below. I first drew a reference sheet and a modeling reference to help with modeling. To model the face, I created a head sculpt and then placed planes above that sculpt. For the hair, I used Nurbs curves and lines to bring the hair to life. After that, I imported the model into Adobe Substance Painter to create materials for the model and then brought it back into Blender for rigging and animation. You can see the results in the last two GIFs.

    Lootbox Projects

    The lootbox was a project to help get familiar with the 3D modeling pipeline. I first created concept art and then made low and high poly versions of the lootbox. Then I used both Photoshop and Substance Painter seperately to create textures. Finally, I brought it into Unity to stage it with lighting. I did this with two different lootbox concepts.
