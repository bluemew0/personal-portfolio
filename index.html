About

I am a 4th year at UC Santa Cruz expected to graduate in June 2023. I am currently studying art & design for games & playable media with a minor in language studies. I am interested in creating colorful art and illustrations for games as well as designing and creating websites. I've always wanted to be able to apply my art in more modern and digital ways and I think these two fields fulfill those interests.
