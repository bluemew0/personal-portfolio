Pencils Down

Tomorrow's exam slipped your mind until the night before! You go to bed, worried how it might go, but even your dreams are filled with thoughts of tomorrow's exams. In your dreams, you fight monsters...? With pencils...? It's a different kind of test, but as you go through this dreamscape, you also meet entities that reflect your anxieties about tests.

A game designed and created with Issy Wong at UC Santa Cruz. This game reflects the anxieties when taking a exam in an RPG-style game. While the title screen was created by Issy, everything else below are art assets I created for the game. Many of the art assets are done by Issy while I programmed and uploaded the game using Construct 3.
