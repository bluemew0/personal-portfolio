Slumber the Sea

Slumber the Sea is a turn-based, combat RPG . Play as 3 aspiring dreamers as they fight against nightmare monsters in a fantastical, shared dream! The twist? Every party member offers a unique resource and playstyle based on different types of JRPGs! Play in frantic and dynamic battles with tons of surprising and strategic teamwork combos using each characters' unique abilities! Will you be able to master your dream career and escape this underwater dream world?

I participated in this game as a 2D artist and I created environment concept art, character portraits, UI boxes, background tile sprite, and a combat diagram. I'm very satisfied with how my environment concept art and character portraits turned out. My initial environment concept art explored different dream settings and after receiving fondness for the underwater setting, I decided to explore that further in combination with urban cityscape to convey a sense of urban escapism and dystopia.
