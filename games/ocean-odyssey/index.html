Ocean Odyssey

Imagine: you're a sushi chef, but your family gets turned into sushi. What would you do? Take on the role of Miyo, the Sushi Chef, and embark on a journey to avenge your fallen loved ones at the hands of the fish invasion in this action-adventure game!

This is my senior capstone project that I worked on as co-producer and 2D artist! Some of contributions include:

  • ✦ Driving art production and leading art direction
  • ✦ Using Trello and Google Suite to organize information about the game
  • ✦ Organizing weekly meetings with my co-producer
  • ✦ Optimizing a production schedule for the art department to follow
  • ✦ Maintaining communication with the other co-producer and departments
  • ✦ Concepting environment setting and style
  • ✦ Illustrating environment assets, including bases, props, and tiles
  • ✦ Creating character assets for King Calamar
  • ✦ Collaborating with other artists to create illustrations
  • ✦ Using Clip Studio Paint to illustrate assets
  • ✦ Using Unity to animate Dr. Krab (skeletal animation) and implement assets
  • ✦ Working remotely and in-person with a team of over 10 people

You can play our game here and follow our Twitter for progress updates here. I will be updating this page as development continues.
